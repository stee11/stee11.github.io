End the lockdown

The ending of all the COVID lockdowns and restrictions is the most important issue in the country right now and that is why ending the lockdowns and restrictions is my only campaign platform. Regardless of your opinions of Trump, Biden, the right, the left, or any other group or issue I ask for your vote as a means of ending the destructive lockdowns.

By government mandate without any due process or any law through a legislature, 40 million people have lost their job and means to provide for themselves and family. Kids have been taken out of school, losing precious time to learn while missing their friends.

Obviously the rate of these diseases did not go down, however, people stopped going to the doctor for care Worst of all, the lockdown did not do anything to stop the virus, in fact, it made things arguably worse.

The death rates for the young are pretty close to 0. A majority of the deaths are those over 75.
